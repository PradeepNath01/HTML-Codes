The Pretiest Lady Aashma Chand

“You are beautiful because you embrace
your individuality and stand tall in your authenticity.
Your inner beauty shines through, captivating hearts and
inspiring others to embrace their own uniqueness.” 

In her eyes, a universe does gleam,
A love so deep, it feels like a dream.
Her smile, a beacon in the darkest night,
With her, everything feels so right.
Her laughter, a melody, sweet and pure,
In her presence, my heart is sure.
I cherish each moment, every day,
With the girl i love, come what may.
Her kindness and grace, a gentle art,
She's the keeper of my hopeful heart.
With her, life's a dance, a song, a rhyme,
Forever together, till the end of time.
